Air Quality Monitoring System

This Air Quality Monitoring System displays the Air Quality Index (AQI) for your current location. The widget provides two key AQI measurements: US AQI and China AQI

US AQI: is a measure used in the United States to indicate how clean or polluted the air is and what associated health effects might be a concern. It ranges from 0 to 500, where lower values indicate better air quality and higher values indicate worse air quality.

China AQI: also known as the Chinese Air Quality Index, is similar but is used in China. It also ranges from 0 to 500 and reflects the level of air pollution, providing information on how the air quality can affect health.

The widget will display these indices along with additional information about pollutants in the air to give you a comprehensive view of the air quality in your area.
